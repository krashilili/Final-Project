JobMarket Summary

JobMarket was the capstone project for our Data Analytics Bootcamp at UT Austin. At a high-level, we employed Python and Pandas for data analysis and JavaScript and Tableau for data visualization.

We used a Kaggle dataset of job postings from the tech job website dice.com. In order to create the latitude and longitude points for our Tableau map, we called to the Google Geolocation API. The Python wordcloud library was used for our wordcloud visualizations and the Available Jobs line chart was created with Plotly.

Enjoy our website and dashboard. Thanks!
